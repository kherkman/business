Data Management

Click anywhere in a quadrant to add a new point. Drag points to move them between quadrants. Click on the text to edit the label. Click 'X' to delete a point. Click on a quadrant headline to edit it. Use the **color picker (the larger square next to the headline)** to change quadrant colors. **Point markers will automatically match the quadrant color.**
